Monday, July 10, 2023


I wasent always the nicest person. I think it came from insecurity, I found a group I could fit in with and all I had to do was have controversial opinions and be inconsiderate and cruel. I had finally found a way out, a way to not be alone. Eventually they pushed me away, discarded. Those I orignally wronged, the people I fit in with didnt forgive me. Its been years and many of the people I care about hate me. Its not their fault, Im not owed anything. I dont deserve second chance but in some ways I have been given one, I guess most of them have forgiven me but the ones who havent cause the most hurt.

I have one person who will invite me out to do things. Im thankful but cant shake the feeling that he does it just to try to make me feel better. I know these kind of thoughs are often irrational but hes done something like that before, and while I forgave him I still fear that everything he does for me is fake and out of the want for me not kill myself.

I hate the feeling of my problems being external. Sometimes things arent my fault, I hate it, sometimes you cant fix things, they will lie eternally broken, without purpose.

Some times I get into dangerous thought pattenrs. Try to convince my self that there is a reason. I start to think things that are unprovable, destructive, dangerous. But I cant shake the insanity. Maybe my purpose, the intention of me being here is to be this way, constantly drifting, alone.

I struggle to commit to things, its not that Im losing intrest, its more that ive lost hope. I try new things, think they are cool, then get instantly discourged, afraid, I try think "will I ever be good at this?" or "how do I know this will make me happy?". I rarely come out of that thought being motivated to continue.

Im kinda losing the ability to figure out what im feeling. Recently I havent been crying as much, which is good but its been replaced with something strange. I know im feeling something alot of the time, sometimes I even act on those feelings, but I sometimes fail to recognize what they are, everythings just kinda... dull.

I was thinking of getting a Commodore Amiga. There just seems to be so much cool software for the things!
